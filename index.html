Parallel Cordinates Plot

Instructions

  • To move dimension - Click on dimension label on the top and drag dimension
  • To delete dimension - Double click on dimension label, unselected data will be in background, if want to remove background data then single click on dimension
  • To include dimension back on the plot again double click on dimension label
  • To explore data hover on data line-- A small label will be displayed
  • To explore data - click on data line - Line will be highlighted with red color - To deselect again click on same line
  • To explore multiple datalines at same time, click on dimension axis and drag to till the line you want to explore
  • To change the default color of all the lines - select color drop from side color box and click on draw button
  • To plot data from other csv files just select csv file from dropdown and wait for a moment
