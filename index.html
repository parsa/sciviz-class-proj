Parallel Cordinates Plot

Instructions

  • To move a dimension, click on dimension label on the top and drag dimension
  • To delete a dimension, double click on dimension label, unselected data will be in background, if want to remove background data then single click on dimension
  • To put a dimension back on the plot, double click on its label
  • To explore data, hover the mouse curser on data line. A small label will be displayed
  • To explore data, click on data line. Line will be highlighted in red. To deselect, click on the same line
  • To explore multiple datalines at same time, click on dimension axis and drag to till the line you want to explore
  • To change the default color of all the lines, select the desired color form the down menu on the side box and then click on the draw button
  • To choose a different data set, select a different CSV file from dropdown. It may take a moment for the graph to display
