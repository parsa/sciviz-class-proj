Parallel Cordinates Visualization

Synopsis

  • To move a dimension, drag the its label on the top to the desired location.
  • To remove a dimension, double click on its label. Unselected data will be shown in a ligher color. To remove background data, click on the dimension label once again.
  • To put a dimension back on the plot, double click on its label.
  • Hover the mouse curser on data line. A small label will be displayed.
  • To select a data line click on it. It will be highlighted in red. To deselect a selected line, click on it.
  • To explore multiple datalines at same time, click on the dimension axis and drag it to the line you want to explore.
  • To change the default color for all lines, select the desired color form the color picker on the right side of the page and then click on the draw button.
  • Choose between available data set by selecting them from the dropdown menu. Depending on the size of the data set and your hardware and browser configuration it may take a few moments for the graph to update
